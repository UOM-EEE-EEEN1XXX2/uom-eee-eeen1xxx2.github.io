Unit delivery plan
------------------
This is a 20 *credit* unit (where a credit is a measure as defined by the `Quality Assurance Agency for Higher Education <https://www.qaa.ac.uk//en/home/>`_. It corresponds to 200 hours of study. In these hours you should carry out a mixture of the assigned tasks and reading materials made provided by us, and self-directed learning carrying out further reading and further practice. 

These study hours are intended to be spread across 12 weeks, matching the duration of a semester at the University of Manchester. The breakdown of study tasks and hours or study each week are given in the table below. Topics are as given :ref:`on the course aims page<topics>`.
